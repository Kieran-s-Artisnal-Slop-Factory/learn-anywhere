Learn Anywhere is a course platform with no accounts and no server. You're
reading this lesson on it right now, and this course teaches the platform
itself — every feature you'll learn about is demonstrated by the course as
you go.

## How a course works

You clicked **Enroll** to get here (or you're about to). [[enrollment|Enrollment]]
copied this entire course — every chapter, lesson, quiz, and test — into your
browser's storage. From now on the course works offline, your progress is
saved on your device, and nothing you do here leaves it unless you
explicitly send it somewhere.

A course is a stack of **chapters**, and each chapter is a list of
**lessons**. You're in chapter 1, lesson 1. The breadcrumbs at the top and
the pager at the bottom keep you oriented; the course page shows everything
at a glance with live progress badges.

## Try the glossary

Some words in lessons look like this: [[indexeddb|IndexedDB]]. That's a
glossary link — hover it (or tap it) for a short definition, and follow
*Read more* if you want the full page. There's also a Glossary entry in the
top navigation listing every term. You'll see these links throughout the
course; they're how unfamiliar terms get explained without derailing the
lesson.

## This is a reading lesson

Lessons come in two kinds, and this one is a *reading* — there's no quiz
attached. When you've read it, press **Mark as read** to complete it and move
on. The next lesson explains the other kind.
