A small demonstration course for the **database exercise** system: lessons
run your SQL against a real SQLite database in the browser, and solutions are
checked against the actual state of the database — not a multiple-choice
answer. Everything works offline after enrollment, like every other course.

*This course exists to exercise the feature during development; replace it
with real content (or fold these patterns into your own courses).*
