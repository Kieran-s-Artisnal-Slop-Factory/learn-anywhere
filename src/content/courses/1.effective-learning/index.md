Learn how to learn. This course covers the science of memory and the study
techniques that research actually supports — [[active-recall|active recall]],
[[spaced-repetition|spaced repetition]], [[interleaving]] — and how to put them to work in your
own studying.

Each chapter mixes short readings with quizzes, and ends with a test covering
the whole chapter.
