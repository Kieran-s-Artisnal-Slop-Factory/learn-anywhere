---
term: Exercise
short: A lesson with a quiz attached. Submitting the quiz completes the lesson; lessons without a quiz are readings, completed with "Mark as read".
---

Every lesson on the platform is one of two kinds, and the kind is never
declared explicitly — it's derived from the content:

- a lesson **with** a `quiz` in its [[frontmatter]] is an *exercise*,
  completed by answering and submitting the quiz (any score completes it;
  the score is recorded);
- a lesson **without** one is a *reading*, completed with a "Mark as read"
  button.

Exercises show the lesson text on the left and the quiz beside it, so you can
answer with the material in view. You can retake a quiz at any time — the new
score replaces the old one.
