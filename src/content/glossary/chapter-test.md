---
term: Chapter test
short: A full-page assessment at the end of a chapter. A chapter with a test only completes once every lesson is done AND the test is submitted.
---

Chapters can end with a test — a full-width page of questions at
`/courses/<course>/<chapter>/test/`, declared in the chapter's
[[frontmatter]] rather than in any lesson.

Tests use the same question types as quizzes, but they gate progress: a
chapter that has a test isn't complete until every lesson is finished *and*
the test has been submitted. The last lesson of the chapter points forward to
the test, and your latest test score is shown on the chapter and course
pages.

Like quizzes, tests can be retaken — the recorded score updates, but once
taken, the completion gate stays open.
